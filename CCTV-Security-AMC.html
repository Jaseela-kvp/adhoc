Comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for Security and Smart Systems

Ensure the longevity and performance of your security and smart home systems with our tailored Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC). Designed for both residential and commercial properties, our AMC services provide regular maintenance, system upgrades, and 24/7 support to keep your systems running efficiently.

Why Choose an AMC?

Regular maintenance is crucial to prevent system failures and optimize performance. With our AMC, you’ll receive:

Preventive Maintenance**: Scheduled inspections to prevent breakdowns and ensure systems run smoothly.

24/7 Support**: Immediate technical assistance and troubleshooting from our expert team.

System Upgrades**: Stay up to date with the latest firmware and software for improved security and performance.

Reduced Downtime**: Minimize disruptions with proactive maintenance and fast repairs.

What We Cover

Our AMCs include comprehensive support for various systems, ensuring peak performance and security:

CCTV Systems: Regular inspection and cleaning of cameras, DVRs, NVRs, and monitoring equipment from brands like Bosch, Hikvision, and Axis.

Alarm Systems: Testing and maintenance of sensors, control panels, and backup power solutions for alarms from Ajax, Honeywell, and Texecom.

Access Control: Regular updates and servicing of biometric, RFID, and key card systems from brands like HID Global and Suprema.

Smart Home Systems: Maintenance of smart lighting, HVAC, and security integrations, compatible with Crestron, Control4, Nest, and Honeywell platforms.

Gate Barriers & Parking Systems: Routine checks and repairs of barriers from FAAC, CAME, and Hormann.

Tailored AMC Packages

We offer flexible AMC packages to suit your property’s unique requirements, whether for a single villa, large residential complex, or commercial site:

Basic: Ideal for small homes, includes quarterly inspections and basic repairs.

Standard: Suited for medium-sized properties, with bi-monthly inspections and 24/7 support.

Premium: Designed for large properties or businesses, offering monthly maintenance, system upgrades, and priority response.

Why AMC with Us?

Certified Technicians: Our team is trained to handle a wide range of security and automation systems, ensuring quick and efficient service.

SIRA Approved: Our maintenance services comply with Abudhabi security standards , ensuring regulatory adherence for high-security environments.

Proactive Service: We detect issues before they become costly problems, saving you time and money in the long run.

ADMCC-Approved Solutions

All our gate barrier systems comply with Abudhabi security standards, ensuring maximum security and regulatory compliance for high-risk zones like banks, warehouses, and critical infrastructure.

By opting for an AMC, you can ensure your systems remain efficient, secure, and up to date, protecting your property from potential failures.
