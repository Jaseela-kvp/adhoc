Smoke Management System Maintenance

Smoke control systems are considered life safety systems and they play a very important role in keeping buildings safe during a fire. By venting smoke out of the building, they help occupants escape safely and also assist firefighters in reaching and putting out the fire quickly.

Regular smoke control system maintenance is a legal requirement under the Regulatory Reform Fire Safety Order 2005. Systems should receive a full service, inspections and testing from a competent person at a quaified and accredited company at least once a year.

Adhoc Light + Air's smoke control system maintenance experts are equipped to perform remedial works and preventative refurbishment to replace older or broken equipment as they carry a comprehensive stock of spare parts and are available 24/7, 365 days a year to respond to system failures and call out requests. This ensures your systems are always compliant and in good working order.

We also offer smoke control and fire damper maintenance.

At Adhoc Fire & Security System , we understand that each building is unique and that is why we tailor our smoke ventilation system maintenance plans to meet your specific needs.

We offer a range of packages to suit all building requirements including both annual and multi-visit annual contracts for smoke control maintenance including smoke shaft systems, smoke vents, smoke curtains, dampers, window actuators, control panels and sensors.
