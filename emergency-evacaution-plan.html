Adhoc offers comprehensive Emergency & Fire Evacuation Plan services to ensure the safety of your property and occupants, fully compliant with Abu Dhabi Civil Defence regulations and informed by the House of Expertise. Our team provides end-to-end solutions that include design, layout, supply, and installation all tailored to fit the specific architecture and requirements of each property.

Our Service Offerings

Consultation & Assessment

  • Initial site visit to understand the property layout, occupancy levels, and unique safety requirements.
  • Consultation to align the evacuation plan with Abu Dhabi Civil Defence standards and other regulatory requirements.

Custom Design & Detailed Drawings

  • Preparation of bespoke evacuation plans designed to optimize safe exit routes based on the building's architecture.
  • Clear, detailed drawings with marked exits, assembly points, and emergency contact details, ensuring compliance with local safety codes.

Plan Layout & Installation

  • Accurate layout and placement of evacuation plans across key areas in the property.
  • Installation of clearly marked signage for emergency exits, fire alarms, and other critical points as per Abu Dhabi Civil Defence and House of Expertise standards.

Regulatory Compliance

  • Assurance that all designs and installations meet current civil defense requirements, providing legal peace of mind for property owners.
  • Continuous coordination with the House of Expertise to incorporate the latest safety insights and comply with expert recommendations.

Ongoing Support

  • Routine checks and updates of evacuation plans to maintain compliance and ensure effectiveness.
  • Responsive service team available to make adjustments or expansions as needed.
