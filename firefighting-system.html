Fire Fighting System

Firefighting is the act of attempting to prevent the spread of and extinguish significant unwanted fires in buildings, vehicles, woodlands, etc. Adhoc fire and safety was approved by Abu dhabi civil defense for carry out these activities in Abu dhabi and Al ain. Our team will helps you from design to success completion of firefighting installation in your building as per NFPA standards and building codes. Our services includes fire fighting pipes installation of GI, HDPE, fire hydrants installation, fire zone control valves, landing valves, air release valves etc. We provide our services for installation and maintenance in low cost without any compromise in quality of works.
