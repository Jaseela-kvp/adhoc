Biometric and RFID Systems: Your Gateway to Accurate Attendance

At Adhoc, we provide advanced Time Attendance Machines designed to streamline employee management and enhance workforce productivity. Whether you run a small business or a large enterprise, our systems offer reliable, real-time tracking for attendance, timekeeping, and labor data, ensuring efficient and accurate records.

Why Choose Our Time Attendance Solutions?

Biometric Precision: Our machines utilize cutting-edge biometric technology, such as fingerprint, facial recognition, and iris scanning, to eliminate time fraud and buddy punching. This ensures secure and accurate attendance data.

Multiple Integrations: Seamlessly integrate our time attendance systems with your payroll software for automated, error-free calculations of work hours, overtime, and leave.

Access Control Integration: Control who enters your facility by combining time attendance systems with access control systems, ensuring security and attendance tracking in one streamlined solution.

Cloud-Based Solutions: Our cloud-enabled devices allow businesses to monitor attendance from anywhere, offering flexibility and convenience to HR teams. Manage shifts, track hours, and generate reports remotely.

Real-Time Notifications: Get instant alerts on any unusual patterns such as late check-ins, absences, or overtime via email or SMS notifications, enabling proactive management of workforce activity.
